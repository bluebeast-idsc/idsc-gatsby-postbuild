    Cars And Coffee Chino Hills | Sundays | Woodview Plaza

    May 23 @ 12:00 am - 2:00 am

    Cars and Coffee Chino Hills  is a super-casual and relaxed event.  Taking place every Sunday it features a mix of muscle, hot rods, customs, antiques, and, newer vehicles.  Anything and everything is welcome and on display.  And it doesn’t matter if the car is finished and perfect or a project-in-the-works.  I noticed a few for sale signs on the cars there too.

    Automotive chit-chat is nearly impossible to avoid.  Everyone is talking up the scene and enjoying each other’s company.

    If you’re in the area or feel like making a drive, this is a great show to hit.

    Pristine Hot Rods & Project Cars

    Chino Hills Cars And Coffee

    Dates: Every Sunday Morning
    Location: Donut Star – 4195 Chinos Hills Parkway – Chino Hills, California
    Starts: 7 AM
    Ends: 9 AM
    Admission: Free
    Vehicle Registration: Free
