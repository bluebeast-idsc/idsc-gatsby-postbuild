    2021 Chevrolet Colorado [ZR2] Review, Prices, Specs & Pics

    Posted by Tom Smith|Nov 02, 2020|Dealers,Martin Chevrolet,News,Reviews

    The 2021 Chevrolet Colorado ZR2 is a beautiful midsize beast with upgrades for this year.

    So, the ZR2 is near Chevy’s top trim, off-road-ready truck.  And while it comes with serious off-road capabilities.  It drives and handles surprisingly smooth on-road too.

    Colorado ZR2’s already rugged and athletic good looks get even better for 2021.  With a new frontend and tailgate, all Colorado trim levels get a light front (and rear) facelifts this year.

    For this review, my friends at Martin Chevrolet in Torrance, CA gave me the gorgeous ZR2 pictured throughout this article.

    2021 Chevy Colorado Top-3:

    1. Exterior style.
    2. Interior comfort.
    3. The Drive.

    I wouldn’t have guessed the 2021 Chevy Colorado continues only the second generation for the vehicle.  But, that’s the case.  Here in the United States, Colorado started with the model year 2004 through ’12 for the first-gen.  And from then until now (so far) for the second generation.

    But with it’s big-brother Silverado being Chevy’s top-selling vehicle.  Midsize Colorado has kept pace with Chevrolet’s excellence in pick-up truck manufacturing.

    2021 Chevrolet Colorado Trims & Pricing

    Chevrolet doesn’t mess around when it comes to trim options.  Because they want to make sure there’s one for every need and everyone.

    2021 Colorado Trims & Starting MSRPs:

    • WT $30,295
    • LT $32,395
    • Z71 $36,695
    • ZR2 $42,795

    So, the above trims are your starting point and then you can go crazy with additional options and accessories.  Because it’s a Chevy truck and that’s what they do. :0)

    Multiple Special Edition Packages

    Cabs & Beds

    But don’t forget, when truck buying, you have two other big factors.  And those are; 1) the type of cab you go with and, 2) the length of bed you select.

    Engines, Transmissions, Towing & MPG

    The 2021 Colorado offers three different powerplants.  And that includes an efficient 4-cylinder, V6 (my choice), and an available turbo-diesel.

    Engines & Estimated Fuel Economy:

     

    2.5-Liter 4-cylinder

    • 200 Horsepower
    • 191 pound-feet of torque
    • 3,500 towing capability
    • 19 MPG city / 25 highway

     

    3.6-Liter V6

    • 308 hp
    • 272 lb-ft of torque
    • 7,000 towing capacity
    • 18 mpg city / 25 highway

     

    2.8-Liter Duramax turbo-diesel

    • 181 hp
    • 369 lb-ft of torque
    • 7,700 towing capacity
    • 18 mpg city / 28 mpg highway
      • Configuration depending.

    Transmissions

    And when it comes to transmissions, of course, you have your optional 2 or 4-wheel drive.

    But additionally, for the 2021 Chevy Colorado, you have the option of an available 6-speed manual transmission or the 8-speed automatic.

    301 hp | V6

    2021 Chevy Colorado Exterior

    Consider that Colorado easily morphs into whatever form and function you’re appetite desires.  Whether that’s beast, beauty, or something in between.

    Crew Cab 140.5″ wheelbase / 224.9″ length

    Special Edition Packages

    The ZR2 package is pictured throughout this article.  And it clearly begs to be taken off the beaten path.  But you can take that off-road-readiness to the next level with the ZR2 Bison package.

    Conversely if on-road is more your truck-driving style then, there are the Redline or the Z71 Midnight offerings.

    And if lean toward a complete blackout the 2021 Chevrolet Colorado also offers the ZR2 Dusk and Midnight editions.

    Look Inside The 2021 Chevrolet Colorado

    There’s no lack of creature comforts inside Chevy Colorado.  Of course the particular options you receive depend largely on the trim you select.

    Class-leading comfort.

    Nevertheless, the space inside Colorado is ample and well designed.  I love the simple user-interface of the center console.  And the logical placement and simple usability of controls on the steering wheel.

    Out-of-sight storage

    Plus smart and versatile storage space under the backseat is always a telltale sign of a well-engineered truck.

    Infotainment & Safety Technology

    Android Auto and Apple CarPlay are both available, along with a 4G LTE Wi-Fi Hotspot.  Plus lots of USB ports and wireless charging and newly introduced Amazon Alexa.

    Simple User-Interface

    Driver Assistance Safety

    The rearview camera is standard.  And available driver assistance features include forward collision alert, lane departure warning, and rear park assistance.

    2021 Chevy Colorado Top-3

    So, my Top-3 reasons to buy the 2021 Chevy Colorado are simple.  And that’s pretty much what a truck should be.  Because it’s as much a life’s tool as it is a vehicle.

    1. Exterior style: Whether your configuration is on-road, off-road, or work truck, Colorado is a great looking truck.
    2. Interior comfort: For a midsize, Colorado is arguably the most comfortable driving vehicle in its class.
    3. The Drive: And speaking of the drive, the 2021 Colorado is a great driver both on and off-road.

     

    62.4″ track / 74.3″ width
